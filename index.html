Chapter II

The 36-hour process

Every Ruby Bagel takes 36 hours from flour to your hands. There are no shortcuts.

01
Hour 0
The Mix
Canadian high-gluten flour. Barley malt syrup from a single Vermont farm. Water filtered seven times.
02
Hour 1-24
Cold Ferment
24-hour cold retard at precisely 38°F. This is where the flavor compounds develop. You can't rush chemistry.
03
Hour 30
The Boil
Honey-malt water, 90 seconds per side. This is the step that separates a bagel from a roll with a hole.
04
Hour 36
Wood-Fired
Cherry wood. 500°F. 14 minutes. The crust shatters. The interior stretches like silk. It's done.
